The Bachelor: What Makes the Perfect Bachelor Contestant?

Looking for your true love? Looking to win your next Bachelor TV show bracket? You've come to the right place!

We, Bachelor Nation, created this site based on web scraped data from the Bachelor TV show. We divided our visualization into 3 sections - Outcomes, Hometowns, and Occupations.
This site gives a breakdown of who won, when, and more information on the aggregate and individual level, so you can find your own insights for that little competitive advantage on that bracket or on that date.

Contestant Hometowns

This visualization depicts the hometowns of Bachelor contestants and can be filtered by each season. Click on a map marker for more information about the contestant or on the layer button to filter out layer groups.

  • Red represents Contestants (eliminated before the halfway point)
  • Orange represents Contestants (eliminated after the halfway point)
  • Green respresents Winners

Season Tree


This visualization depicts which contestants were eliminated each week for seasons of the Bachelor.
Choose a season to visualize the contestants and their outcomes.

Bubble Chart of Occupations

This visualization depicts the contestants' job categories in all seasons. The size of the bubble indicates frequency, and a darker color indicates a better average performance. Click on a job category to learn more about the job titles in it.
