The Bachelor: Finding your perfect match!

Looking for your true love? Looking to win your next Bachelor TV show bracket? You've come to the right place!

We, Bachelor Nation, created this site based on web scraped data from the Bachelor TV show. We divided our visualization into 3 sections - Outcomes, Hometowns, and Occupations.
This site gives a breakdown of who won, when, and more information on the aggregate and individual level, so you can find your own trends and insights for that little competitive advantage on that bracket or on that date.

Contestant Occupations

This visualization depicts the job categories of Bachelor contestants in all of the seasons. The size of the bubble indicates frequency, while the color indicates the Average Elimination Week.
